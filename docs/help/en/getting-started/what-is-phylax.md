# What is Phylax

!!! info "Placeholder (Iteration 1)"
    This page is part of the Stage 1 scaffolding. Full content will follow in a later iteration.

Phylax is a personal, data-sovereign health platform built around the concept of **living health**: your medical profile is not a folder of files but a living document that grows with you.

## Planned content for this page

- The idea of the living profile
- Why local-first and zero-knowledge
- What Phylax is NOT (no diagnoses, no therapy recommendations, not a medical device)
- How it differs from classic health apps and from electronic patient records
